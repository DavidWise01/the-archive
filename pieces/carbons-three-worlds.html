FIVE WORLDS
ONE TIMING ENGINE · FIVE WAYS TO PLAY IT
a retro game-manual saga — conflict, resolution, judgment, the realms beyond… and the building of a better world

It began as a trilogy on a single beat-the-meter engine, turned inside out three times. In FIST CITY you throw the punch; in PEACE CITY you refuse to; in SCORE CITY you stand in the middle and make the call.

Then the saga steps beyond the everyday into a second epoch. KOMBAT REALMS takes that same heart into a supernatural tournament where realms settle everything by single kombat — bloodless, but big. And UTOPIA turns the engine fully to the good: eight real figures from history take on the great trials of civilization, and every breakthrough builds a brighter world.

ONE ENGINE, FIVE STANCES
the same beat-the-meter loop, pointed five different directions
FIST · ATTACK

Time the meter to strike. Drive their health bar to zero. A PERFECT lands a critical.

PEACE · CALM

Same meter — talk their tension down to zero while guarding your composure. A PERFECT is a breakthrough.

SCORE · JUDGE

Same meter — officiate the match to a clean finish while protecting your credibility.

KOMBAT · BANISH

Same meter — but realms are at stake. Empty their life and a PERFECT finisher banishes them home.

UTOPIA · BUILD

Same meter — turned to the good. Overcome a trial and a PERFECT breakthrough advances Utopia.

Every world shares the deep stuff: a tiered ranking ladder, illustrated character files, five arcade modes (a career run, divisions/realms/ages, a free exhibition, a no-stakes practice, and an 8-seat tournament), difficulty and best-of-3, plus a wholesome juniors division and friendly cup with medals for everyone and no sore losers.
FIVE WORLDS is an original parody saga. The styles, disciplines, officials, kombatants, champions, and opponents of the first four games are fictional and affectionate — and no one ever gets hurt. UTOPIA's eight founders are real historical figures, honored factually for their real contributions; its trials are symbolic.
