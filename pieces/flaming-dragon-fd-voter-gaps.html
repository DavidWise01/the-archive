Gaps That Prevent Eligible Citizens From Voting
GAP-001Poison Postcard Purge
SUPPRESSIONDISINFORMATION
Voters mailed postcards that look like junk mail. Non-return = purge. Arizona return rate: 10%. Georgia: barely 1%. Result: 4,776,706 voters wrongly purged before 2024 per EAC data. Wisconsin purged 166,433 — Harris lost the state by 29,397. Pennsylvania purged 360,132 — three times Trump's victory margin.
EAC data · Multiple state records · Nashville PRIDE / SD Voice & Viewpoint analysis
GAP-002Vigilante Voter Challenges
SUPPRESSION
For first time since 1946, self-proclaimed "voter-fraud hunters" challenged 317,886 voters by August 2024. In Georgia alone, challenges exceeded 200,000. One person in Savannah challenged 900 voters. True the Vote projected 2 million challenges by Election Day. No evidence of fraud required to challenge.
NAACP Georgia · True the Vote filings · League of Women Voters tracking (64 anti-voter lawsuits in 2024)
GAP-003Mail Ballot Disqualification
SUPPRESSIONADA WEAPONIZATION
2,121,000 mail-in ballots disqualified for minor clerical errors (postage due, signature mismatch). 585,000 in-person ballots disqualified. 1,216,000 provisional ballots rejected. Black voters 400% more likely to have mail ballot rejected (Washington state audit). Disabled voters use mail at 10 points higher than non-disabled.
EAC data · Washington state audit · MIT Election Lab
GAP-004Registration Rejection Pipeline
SUPPRESSION
3.24 million new registrations rejected or not entered on rolls in time to vote in 2024. SAVE Act would add in-person citizenship proof requirement that 21.3 million citizens can't meet. Election workers face 5-year prison for registering without documents — chilling effect.
EAC · Brennan Center · Campaign Legal Center
GAP-005Polling Place Inaccessibility
SUPPRESSIONADA WEAPONIZATION
73% of polling places non-compliant nationally (GAO 2008). Harris County: 699/701 non-compliant. Chicago: <10% compliant. Detroit: 84% with barriers. Result: 15.3 million disabled eligible voters did not vote in 2022. Self-care disability turnout: 48.1% vs 66% non-disabled.
GAO-08-442T · Houston Chronicle · Chicago Board of Elections · Detroit Disability Power / Carter Center · Census 2024
GAP-006Mail Voting Ban (Proposed)
SUPPRESSIONADA WEAPONIZATION
MEGA Act bans universal mail-in voting in 8 states + DC. Bans "ballot harvesting" by non-family — meaning caregivers, group home staff, disability advocates can't return ballots. Requires mail ballots arrive by Election Day (no postmark grace). Disabled voters' primary voting method eliminated. States that expanded mail voting saw 7-point rise in disabled turnout.
House Administration Committee · Votebeat · MIT Election Lab
GAP-00790-Day Quiet Period Violations
SUPPRESSIONSYSTEM MANIPULATION
NVRA prohibits systematic voter removal within 90 days of election. Virginia purged 1,600 voters within this window in 2024. Supreme Court allowed it on emergency appeal despite federal judge finding it illegal. Alabama purge halted by court. Multiple states violated the 90-day rule with no penalty.
Supreme Court · League of Women Voters · Campaign Legal Center · Al Jazeera
GAP-008Error-Prone Database Matching
SUPPRESSIONSYSTEM MANIPULATION
SAVE system and voter roll matching use flawed criteria. Florida 2000: thousands wrongfully purged on bad matching. Officials "cobble together" data from DMVs and unreliable federal sources.
NPR · EAC · Campaign Legal Center · Brennan Center
