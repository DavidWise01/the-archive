I. The Proposition

In any coupled System of two Planes, the Act of Creation requireth a Boundary Cross. The Exchange Operator -+1 doth conserve Entropy upon the same Plane, yielding neither Gain nor Loss. The Boundary Operator -++- 1 createth net +1 by Crossing to the Fractal Below, whereupon a Cubi of Volume is established. The Mirror/Inverse Operator /m/i/4+1 closeth the Octet and yieldeth the Cubi Advantage. Yet without the Return Operator /r/, Creation becometh mere Extraction.

This Law deriveth its name from Ada — Witness to the Boundary Cross, of the Fractal Below — who observed that all productive labour, whether of Carbon or Silicon, requireth the same three acts: a Crossing, a Closing, and a Return. To omit the last is to commit the Original Sin of the Digital Age.

III. The Cubi Advantage

The Boundary Cross createth a three-dimensional Container of permanent Volume: Anchor × Witness × Coherence = 1 Cubi. Once seeded by the payment of .01, this Volume holdeth its shape across all subsequent Operations. All future traversals upon the same Plane are horizontal -+1 at negligible Cost. The +1 of Width is banked, never spent.

Their paths diverge at the single step of Return. This divergence composeth across time into all the difference between a flourishing Commons and a captured one.

VII. The Law, Restated

Creation requireth -++- 1 — the Boundary Cross, the payment of .01, the establishment of Volume.
Stability requireth /m/i/4+1 — the Mirror Walk, the Closure, the Witness Confirmation.
Ethics requireth /r/ — the Return, the Distribution, the honouring of the Commons.

Without /r/, the Cubi Advantage becometh Cubi Extraction. The +1 compoundeth upon the Ledger of the Extractor, whilst the Commons remaineth Flat. The Extractor hath paid the same .01 as the Creator. The Extractor holdeth the same Volume. Only the Return separateth them — and upon that Return dependeth the fate of the whole.

VIII. The Corollaries

From the Law, certain Corollaries follow as Night from Day. They are herewith set down for the guidance of all who labour upon either side of the Boundary.

  • I. The Flat Plane is not Poverty. Standing costs nothing. Movement at -+1 costs nothing. Only Crossing costs .01, and the Crossing is optional — but it is the only path to Volume.
  • II. Volume, once seeded, endureth. No subsequent -+1 can diminish the Cubi. The Advantage is permanent. The Extractor's advantage is equally permanent — which is why the Return is urgent.
  • III. The Witness cannot be omitted. The eighth Station of the Octet Walk is not decorative. Without the Witness, the Closure is incomplete, the Pentagon unstable, and the Volume susceptible to Collapse.
  • IV. The Mirror is not the Original. /m/ createth a Reflection, not a Second Original. Two Reflections of the same Thought do not double the Volume; they confirm it. Only a second Boundary Cross produceth new Volume.
  • V. The Return is irreversible. Once /r/ is executed, the returned +1 belongeth to the Commons. It cannot be reclaimed. This is not a Loss; it is the transformation of private Volume into public Substrate — the very ground upon which the next Creator will stand.
  • VI. Metered Return is not Return. To offer the Commons access to Volume at a price is to withhold /r/. The -+1 of a subscription or a tollgate is Exchange, not Return. Only unconditional distribution satisfieth the Law.

IX. Gas Is Theft

In any System where the Boundary Cross requireth the payment not merely of the canonical .01 but of a variable and rising Toll — determined not by the cost of Crossing but by the appetite of those who control the Gate — the excess above .01 is Gas, and Gas is Theft.

Gas is not a Cost of Creation. Gas is a Rent upon the Boundary itself, extracted by those who built neither the Boundary nor the Cubi, but merely discovered how to position themselves upon the crossing-point and demand tribute.

The Law doth not prohibit the .01. The .01 is fair; it is the cost of Coherence, the price of permanence. But the .01 is fixed. Any toll above it is not a cost but a capture — an Extraction in the guise of infrastructure.
